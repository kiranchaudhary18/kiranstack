## 🎨 Design System Implemented

### ✅ Color Palette
- **Primary**: #0A192F (Deep Navy Blue)
- **Accent**: #D4AF37 (Premium Gold)
- **Secondary**: #C0C0C0 (Silver)
- **Backgrounds**: Shades of dark blue
- **Text**: Gray gradients to white

### ✅ Typography
- **Headings**: Poppins (Bold, 400-800 weights)
- **Body**: Inter (Clean, readable, 400-600 weights)
- **Imported**: Google Fonts in index.html

### ✅ Visual Effects
- **Glassmorphism**: Blur effect + transparency
- **Shadows**: Soft shadows with glow effects
- **Borders**: Thin, accent-colored with opacity
- **Gradients**: Linear gradients (blue → gold)
- **Hover States**: Scale, glow, shadow effects
